About

Cafe' Heroina and B&B

Cafe' Heroina B & B is a warm, welcoming Bed & Breakfast. The combined with the warm and friendly welcome will ensure that your stay with us is a truly memorable one. At Cafe' Heroina B&B we will do everything in our power to ensure that your stay with us is an enjoyable home from home experience. If you require any help with your journey we will be only too glad to be your help. Cafe' Heroina is conveniently located for touring all of the attractions in the Bohol. Many guests check in for one night accommodation when making day trips to the Sagbayan Peak, Chocolate Hills, and other tourist spots in Bohol to avoid the unnecessary hassle of unpacking.

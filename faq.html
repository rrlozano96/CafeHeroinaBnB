Frquently Asked Quesions

What are the Payment Methods Available?
Cash, after Checked-in.
What is the standard check-in time?
Regular check in time is between 12pm and 6pm. It is requested that you notify us if you intend to arrive outside these hours.
Is Parking Available at the B&B?
Yes. There is free private car park or off street car park for our guests located to our B&B.
Can I Reserve a Parking Space?
We do not have any reservation policy in place for parking. However our B&B has sufficient parking space for every guest.
Can I Book Breakfast Online? What Does it Cost?
There is no need to book a breakfast online but you can call or email our reception if you require. The cost of the breakfast is included into the price of the room.
Can I Book the Tours Online?
The tours can only be booked in person at our reception.
Are the Rates per Person or per Room?
All prices, unless stated otherwise, are on a per-room-basis.
